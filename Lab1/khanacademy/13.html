Classic Spaghetti Carbonara

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Spaghetti400g
Pancetta150g
Eggs3
Parmesan Cheese50g, grated
Garlic1 clove
Salt and PepperTo taste

Step 1: Cook spaghetti in salted boiling water until al dente.

Step 2: Fry pancetta with garlic. Remove garlic when browned.

Step 3: Beat eggs and Parmesan in a bowl, then add to the drained spaghetti with pancetta. Mix quickly.

Step 4: Season with salt and pepper and serve immediately.

Homemade Margherita Pizza

  • Time: 1 hour 20 minutes (including dough rising)
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Pizza Dough500g
Tomato Sauce200ml
Mozzarella Cheese150g
Fresh BasilA few leaves
Olive Oil2 tbsp
SaltTo taste

Step 1: Preheat your oven to 250°C (480°F). Stretch out pizza dough on a baking tray.

Step 2: Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough, then add slices of mozzarella.

Step 3: Bake for 10-15 minutes until the crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.

Step 4: Garnish with fresh basil leaves and a drizzle of olive oil before serving.
